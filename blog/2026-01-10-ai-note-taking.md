Let's be honest - reading through 20 pages of lecture notes the night before an exam isn't fun. That's why we built an AI-powered summarizer directly into Illinotes.

## The Challenge of Information Overload

Modern students are drowning in content:

- Hours of recorded lectures
- Hundreds of pages of textbook readings
- Slides, handouts, and supplementary materials

How do you distill all of that into what actually matters for your exam?

## How Our Summarizer Works

Our AI summarizer uses advanced language models to:

1. **Identify key concepts** - It recognizes the most important topics in your notes
2. **Create structured summaries** - Organized output with clear headings
3. **Highlight definitions** - Important terms are called out
4. **Generate study questions** - Test yourself on the material

## Tips for Getting the Best Results

Here's how to get the most out of the summarizer:

### 1. Clean Up Your Input
Remove headers, footers, and page numbers from copied text. The cleaner your input, the better your summary.

### 2. Summarize by Topic
Instead of dumping an entire semester's notes, summarize one lecture or one chapter at a time.

### 3. Review and Edit
AI is a tool, not a replacement for understanding. Read through your summary and add your own annotations.
