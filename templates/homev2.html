Everything you need to share and find notes.

Simple tools designed to help UIUC students collaborate effectively.

Post & Edit Notes

Create notes with titles, authors, and course tags. Edit or delete your posts anytime to keep information current.

Smart Filtering

Filter notes by class (CS124, MATH221, etc.), author, or date range. Sort by recency, title, or author name.

Keyword Search

Search through note titles and content to quickly find exactly what you need for homework or exam prep.

Course Organization

Notes are automatically organized by course. Supports CS, MATH, ENG, RHET, PHY, and more UIUC classes.

Time-Based Views

View notes from today, this week, this month, or all time. Perfect for reviewing recent lectures.

Anonymous Posting

Share notes anonymously if you prefer, or add your name to build a reputation as a helpful contributor.

Why we built this.

We're a group of UIUC students who got tired of scrolling through endless GroupMe messages trying to find that one set of notes someone shared last week. Class notes shouldn't disappear into chat history. They should be organized, searchable, and accessible to everyone who needs them.

  • Centralized place for all course notes instead of scattered chats
  • Built with Flask and designed specifically for UIUC courses
  • No ads, no paywalls—just students helping students
  • Created as a CS124 Honors group project (FA25-Group10)

How students use it

  • Step 1

  • Step 2

  • Step 3

  • Step 4

    Filter by class to see all notes for upcoming tests
