Support & Help

We're here to help

How do I share notes?

Simply navigate to the Notes Feed and click the "Create Note" button. Fill in the course code, title, and content, then click "Post" to share your notes with the community.

Is Illinotes really free?

Yes! Illinotes is completely free with no paywalls or premium tiers. We believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone, and we're committed to keeping the platform free forever.

How do I use the AI summarizer?

Visit the AI Tutor page and paste your lecture notes into the text box. Click "Summarize" and our AI will generate a concise summary in seconds. It's perfect for reviewing lengthy notes before exams.

Can I edit or delete my notes?

Absolutely! You can edit or delete any notes you've posted. Just navigate to your profile page and select the note you want to modify. Click "Edit" to update the content or "Delete" to remove it permanently.
