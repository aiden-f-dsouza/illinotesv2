Everything you need to share and find notes.

Simple tools designed to help UIUC students collaborate effectively.

User Accounts & Profiles

Create an account to post, edit, and manage your notes. Track your contributions and build your reputation as a helpful study resource for classmates.

File Attachments

Upload PDFs, images, documents, and presentations alongside your notes. Share complete study materials including slides, diagrams, and practice problems.

Likes & Comments

Like helpful notes and discuss content through comments. Use @mentions to notify specific users and collaborate on study materials.

Smart Filtering & Search

Filter notes by course, date, tags, or search keywords. Sort by most recent, most liked, or most commented to find the best study resources quickly.

Note Summarizer

Paste lengthy lecture notes or textbook excerpts and get concise summaries. Perfect for quick reviews before exams.

Hashtags & Tagging

Organize notes with custom tags and hashtags. Filter by topics like #midterm, #algorithms, or #proofs to find exactly what you need.

Why we built this.

We're a group of UIUC students who got tired of scrolling through endless GroupMe messages trying to find that one set of notes someone shared last week. Class notes shouldn't disappear into chat history. They should be organized, searchable, and accessible to everyone who needs them.

This platform was born from real frustration and built to solve a real problem. We wanted something that felt permanent, trustworthy, and actually useful—not just another disposable tool.

  • Centralized place for all course notes instead of scattered chats
  • Built with Flask and designed specifically for UIUC courses
  • No ads, no paywalls—just students helping students learn better
  • Created as a CS124 Honors group project (FA25-Group10)

How students use it

  • Step 1

    Browse notes after missing a lecture to catch up on key concepts

  • Step 2

    Post study guides before exams to help classmates prepare

  • Step 3

    Search for specific topics when working on homework assignments

  • Step 4

    Filter by class to see all notes relevant to upcoming tests
